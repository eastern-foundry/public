Inside the Foundry

Empowering Federal Innovators

We believe that innovative solutions address our country's toughest challenges. However, the slow and complex procurement process prevents such ideas, products, and services from being found and utilized by the federal government.

Eastern Foundry was created to solve this problem. We provide our members with affordable co-working and office space, valuable business services, and a community of experienced mentors and partners dedicated to helping them enter and succeed in government procurement.

First of a kind

The infrastructure you need

We structure our space based on what our members need. We offer coworking spaces, large & small private offices, event space, and conference rooms equipped with full A/V.

The service you want

We extend a variety of services to our members, including: PR / marketing, design, event planning, service & product discounts, and client development unique to each company in our community.

The community you seek

We provide a place where like-minded companies convene to learn from each other. Our members not only benefit from the insight of fellow Eastern Foundry members, but from mentors with extensive background in business and government.
