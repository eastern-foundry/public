What does Eastern Foundry offer beyond office space?

Eastern Foundry provides access to our membership network, educational events and trainings focused on federal procurement and professional services, including legal and accounting advice, graphic design services and more.

Do I need to be a veteran to become a member of Eastern Foundry?

No, Eastern Foundry is comprised of business owners of all backgrounds (some veteran and some civilian) and our member companies value the unique perspectives we all bring to the table.

Who are Eastern Foundry’s members and how do I connect with them?

Members of Eastern Foundry span sectors but all seek do conduct business with the federal government. As a member, you gain access to the directory of Eastern Foundry member company profiles. Additionally, each month you can meet our members at our Happy Hour events.

Can Eastern Foundry help me identify teaming partners for government contracts?

Yes, Eastern Foundry assists our member companies with identifying teaming partners through our membership portal. We also publish our members' capability statements for easy viewing and partnering.

How can Eastern Foundry help me win government contracts?

Eastern Foundry regularly posts opportunities from agencies and shares them with our membership base. In addition, our expert-led Academy courses provide in-depth information on the procurement process – from administration to proposal writing and much more – helping you get the knowledge you need to win your first contracts faster.
